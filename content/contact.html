Most of team members are available for questions on Ethereum Classic Slack. If you have any questions it's better to join Slack and ask questions in public channel #development. Someone from the community or ETCDEV Team will answer that question.

Ethereum Classic is a public community, and we'll appreciate if you'll ask questions only in public channels, and only consider private messages only on special topics. Such as security issues with the protocol, or similar.
