Most of the ETC Community are chatting on ETC Discord. If you have any questions it's better to join Discord and ask questions in public channel #development. Someone from the ETC community or ETCDEV Team will answer that question.

Ethereum Classic is a public community, and we'll appreciate if you'll ask questions only in public channels, and only consider private messages only on special topics. Such as security issues with the protocol, or similar.

If you are looking for a public communication channel more focused on ETCDEV development, please join us at Gitter: https://gitter.im/etcdev-public
